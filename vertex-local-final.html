This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when you submit the business registration form on this website.

2. Information We Collect

When you complete and submit the registration form on this site, we collect the following information about your business:

  • Business owner name
  • Business name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (optional)
  • Business address
  • Trade or service category
  • Business hours
  • Website URL (if applicable)
  • Social media handles (if provided)

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To create and set up a Google Business Profile on your behalf, as authorized by you
  • To list your business in the Vértex Local verified directory
  • To contact you regarding your profile setup, verification postcard, and ongoing management

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose beyond the services you authorized.

4. How We Store Your Information

Submissions from this form are transmitted to and stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to Vertex Local LLC. Form submissions are processed through Formspree, a secure form processing service, solely for the purpose of delivering your data to our internal records. Formspree does not retain, sell, or share your data. Your information is not stored in this website or on any public server.

5. We Do Not Sell or Share Your Information

Vertex Local LLC does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information or business information with any third party for any commercial purpose. Your information is not shared with advertisers, data brokers, marketing firms, or any outside organization. The only parties who access your submission data are Vertex Local LLC and the internal tools used to deliver it to our records (Formspree, Google Sheets).

6. Google Business Profile

By submitting this form you authorize Vertex Local LLC to create a Google Business Profile using your business information. That profile is governed by Google's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. You are the primary owner of that profile. Vertex Local LLC is added only as a manager. You may remove our access at any time through your Google account.

7. Your Rights Under Texas Law

Under the Texas Business & Commerce Code § 521.002, you have the right to know what personal identifying information we hold about your business and to request its deletion. To make such a request, contact us directly at the email address below. We will respond within a reasonable time and remove your information from our records upon verified request.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access. Submissions are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Access to our internal records is restricted to Vertex Local LLC personnel only.

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of this site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
